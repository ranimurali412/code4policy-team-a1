About Us

Rani Murali is an experienced data analyst with a background in climate and energy policy. She has experience working in a variety of industries from non-profit to federal to professional services consulting. She specializes in energy data analysis and greenhouse gas emissions accounting from energy sources. She is currently pursuing a Master's in Public Policy from the Harvard Kennedy School. While completing her studies she is also working for Pacific Northwest National Lab on grid planning for large-scale decarbonization of electric power and transportation.

Rose Khattar is an economist dedicated to ending sexist oppression. Currently, she is a Master’s in Public Policy student at the Harvard Kennedy School where she serves as Co-Editor-in-Chief of the Women's Policy Journal. In 2019-2020, Rose was also chair of the HKS’ Women’s Caucus and a Research Assistant with the Women and Public Policy Program. Prior to HKS, she worked as an economist in the civil service and as a policy advisor for an Australian senator.

Amanda Said is committed to dismantling structural inequality in education systems.
