US Climate Stimulus Implications

The Biden administration has shared plans of a US Climate Stimulus package which can stimuluate the post-Covid economy, and generate millions of good quality green jobs.

Green jobs will go to white men unless something changes

Using jobs data from The Sierra Club, and matching this with BLS industry data, the chart shows that 9 million out of 10 million jobs created by investment in climate related stimulus will go to men, and just 1 million jobs will go to women.
