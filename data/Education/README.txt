Data source: https://www.bls.gov/emp/tables/educational-attainment.htm

Process:

1. Obtained list of 30 clean energy jobs from Brookings institution report: https://www.brookings.edu/research/advancing-inclusion-through-clean-energy-jobs/

2. Manually filtered the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) 2018 Educational Attainment by Occupation data to include just those jobs. 

3. Identified typical level of educational attainment for those jobs. (“Clean Energy Educational Attainment.xlsx”)

4. Input that into Datawrapper and created pie chart of typical educational attainment needed for clean energy jobs.
