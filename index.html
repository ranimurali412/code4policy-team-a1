How inclusive is the green economy?

Since men are not equals in white supremacist, capitalist, patriarchal class structure, which men do women want to be equal to? — bell hooks



President-elect Joe Biden's comprehensive climate plan calls for a $2 trillion investment, creating upwards of 10 million American jobs.

This website investigates who gets those jobs by looking at:


  • Gender and racial disparities in climate-related industries
  • Access to education required for green jobs
  • Projected job growth from various climate stimulus policies
  • Implications for policymakers to design more inclusive climate policies
